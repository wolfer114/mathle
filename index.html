How to Play

Use the available numbers and math operations to reach the target number.

  • You have 6 guesses to hit the target exactly
  • Use +, -, *, / and parentheses ( )
  • Each available number can only be used once
  • You don't have to use all numbers

Feedback after each guess:

  • Green = exact match!
  • Yellow = close (within 5)
  • Orange = warm (within 20)
  • Gray = far off
Example: 25 * (7 + 3) + 50

A new puzzle appears every day at midnight!
